Account Deletion

If you have created an account in PadelScore using Firebase Authentication (email or Google Sign-In), you can permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to delete your account

  • Open the PadelScore app.
  • Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • Confirm deletion. This will remove your account and associated data stored in Firebase.
